01. About Me.

I'm Suryaraj Bhaduri, a final year student from Meghnad Saha Institute of Technology pursuing Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Ever since I embarked on my programming journey, I've been amazed by the prowess of code and what we can acheive through programming.

I believe programming to me is a superpower which permits me to be as creative as I can get. I also enjoy doing programming contests. Recently I developed a knack towards web development as well.

I strive to bring energy, technical knowledge, and a strong desire to learn with me wherever I go.
